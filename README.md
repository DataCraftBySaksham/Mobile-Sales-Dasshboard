# Mobile-Sales-Dashboard
An interactive dashboard that visualizes mobile sales data, highlighting key metrics such as total sales, total transactions, top-performing brands, and regional performance for data-driven insights.

## Purpose
The Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard is a visually engaging and data-driven Power BI report designed to help users analyze and compare mobile phone sales performance across multiple regions in India. The dashboard highlights key sales metrics such as total revenue, units sold, top-performing brands and models, regional demand patterns, and monthly sales trends. This tool is intended for use by sales analysts, business managers, market researchers, and data-driven decision-makers who seek to understand consumer behavior, track performance, and identify growth opportunities in the Indian mobile market.
